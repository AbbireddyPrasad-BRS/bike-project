KAWASAKI NINJA H2R

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is a track-only motorcycle, renowned for being one of the fastest and most powerful bikes with a supercharged 998cc engine producing over 300 horsepower. It features advanced aerodynamics with carbon fiber bodywork and wings, reaching speeds over 200 mph. Designed for closed-course competition, the H2R showcases Kawasaki's engineering excellence and pursuit of high performance.

MTT 400 RACE READY

The MTT 420RR is a turbine-powered hyperbike, equipped with a Rolls Royce Allison 250-C20 gas turbine engine producing 420 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque. Known for its incredible performance, it features an aluminum alloy frame, carbon fiber fairings, and a 2-speed automatic transmission. Weighing around 500 lbs, it offers a unique and thrilling riding experience !
